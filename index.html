How it works

Once you have successfully selected your genre along with any sub-genre questions (if applicable), you're given a playlist curated and made specifically for that specific genre / sub genre.

You're given the options of playing the playlist on
a) spotify (app / webapp)
b) my personally developed web music player
and you're allowed to choose either of the above and listen to your playlist seamlessly without any hassle.

We're currently working on increasing our variety and number of playlists for each (sub)genre and also working out means to keep your music taste in mind and give you personally curated playlists based on your personal music taste.

We're also working on adding additional streaming services which will be able to support these playlists like apple music, deezer, tidal, soundcloud etc. so please stay tuned (get it? ha) for that. Happy listening !

If you want a specific genre be added to our list, please email them to us! If you have any playlists that goes well along with that genre, please send that in as well.

If you're an artist who wants their music to be added in these playlists for gaining a wider audience etc. please email us along with the playlist genres you'd like your music to be added to.

You can download our apk android app (webviewcontroller) as well for easier access or simply bookmark this website on your browser / add to homescreen to be able to use it anytime. If you like this app, please consider sharing it with your friends, family, colleague, classmates etc.
Please note that our app and website are both 100% safe and we will never reveal any information or data you choose to share with us (no data required currenty) to any third party company / ourselves use it for any unfair means.
